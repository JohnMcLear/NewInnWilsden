Welcome

Your Village Local

⚜

Nestled in the heart of the charming West Yorkshire village of Wilsden, The New Inn has been at the centre of community life for generations.

Whether you're stopping in for a perfectly poured pint of Yorkshire ale after a walk on the moors, or catching the big match on our big screen – there's always a warm welcome waiting for you.

We're proud to be a proper village pub: no airs and graces, just great beer, great company, and a roaring fire in the winter months.

Drinks

A great selection of real ales, craft lagers, ciders, wines and spirits – just pull up a stool.

On The Bar

  • Timothy Taylor's Landlord Yorkshire's finest pale ale – crisp, hoppy, unmistakable
  • Black Sheep Best Bitter A full-bodied, fruity bitter from Masham, North Yorkshire
  • Rotating Guest Ales A changing selection of hand-pulled ales from local breweries
  • Craft Lagers & Ciders A wide range of keg beers, ciders and soft drinks
  • Wines & Spirits House wines, premium spirits and cocktails
